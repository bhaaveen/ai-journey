# My Prompt Library

## Week 1 — Learning prompts

### Explain a concept
**Prompt:** "Explain [concept] like I'm 18 years old and curious"
**Works best for:** Any new AI topic
**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
**Notes:** Add "use an analogy" for better results

### Self-quiz
**Prompt:** "Quiz me on [topic] — 5 questions, then grade me"
**Works best for:** After reading/watching something

Step 1 — Learn:
"Explain [what a transformer is] simply. Use one analogy. Max 150 words."

Step 2 — Go deeper:
"Now explain the mechanism behind it. What's actually happening technically?"

Step 3 — Test yourself:
"Quiz me on [what a transformer is] with 5 questions. Wait for my answers before revealing correct ones."
